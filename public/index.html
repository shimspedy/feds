About Federal Employee Pay Scales

FedsPay.com provides accurate and up-to-date information on federal employee compensation across all 50 states and U.S. territories. Our comprehensive database includes:

  • General Schedule (GS) pay tables by locality
  • Law Enforcement Officer (LEO) salary information
  • Step increases and grade comparisons
  • Annual, hourly, and overtime rates

All data is current for the 2025 fiscal year and reflects official government pay scales.
